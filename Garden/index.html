The Garden of Earthly Delights

Hieronymus Bosch, c. 1490–1510

This triptych is one of the most enigmatic works in art history. It portrays the progression of sin: from the pure beginnings in Eden, through the chaotic indulgences of the world, to the final, terrifying punishments of Hell.
